Music Nostalgia

"Music nostalgia" was my final project of my 4th year of studying in RMMT.

We needed to make a 2-3 min animation, add sound design and do a small study of our chosen topic.

I was exploring the theme how music affects our possibility to remember specific moments and people.

Made in Krita, Adobe Photoshop.
